## Why Do We Need Food?

We eat food because our body needs it to **grow, stay strong, and stay healthy**. Food gives us the **energy** to run, play, study, and do all our daily activities. Without food, we would feel weak and fall sick.

We eat and drink many different things. Some foods are:

- **Sweet** -- like mango, jaggery, and sweets
- **Sour** -- like lemon, tamarind, and amla
- **Spicy** -- like green chillies and pepper
- **Salty** -- like pickles and namkeen
- **Bitter** -- like karela (bitter gourd) and neem

> **Chhappan Bhog** is a special offering of **56 food items** prepared during festivals in India. It represents all six tastes -- sweet, spicy, astringent, sour, salty, and bitter!

## The Story of Shirin the Runner

**Shirin** was a Class 3 student who loved running. She ran faster than everyone in her class! One day, she met a champion runner and asked, "How can I run like you and win medals?"

The champion said, "You must **eat healthy food** and practise a lot." Shirin said she often fell sick. The champion found out that Shirin was eating only **rice and potatoes**.

The champion advised her: "Eating rice and potatoes is good, but you must eat **many different kinds of food** too!"

Shirin started eating:

- Different **vegetables and fruits**
- Foods made from different **grains** -- rice, ragi, jowar, wheat, bajra
- **Pulses** (dal) and **nuts**
- Food **cooked at home**

After a few months, Shirin felt **energetic and healthy**. She ran faster than ever and won a medal for her school!

> Eating a **variety of foods** gives our body all the nutrients it needs. This is called a **balanced diet**.

## What Should We Eat?

A healthy meal should include different types of food:

| Food Group | Examples | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| **Grains** | Rice, wheat, ragi, jowar, bajra | Gives us energy |
| **Vegetables** | Spinach, carrots, potatoes, beans | Keeps us strong and healthy |
| **Fruits** | Mango, apple, guava, banana | Gives vitamins and keeps us well |
| **Pulses (Dal)** | Moong, toor, masoor, chana | Helps our body grow |
| **Milk and dairy** | Milk, curd, paneer, lassi | Makes our bones and teeth strong |
| **Nuts and dry fruits** | Almonds, cashews, groundnuts | Gives energy and good fats |

## Food for Different Seasons

In India, we eat different foods in different seasons because our body needs different things:

| Season | What We Eat and Drink | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Summer** | Kulfi, ice cream, tender coconut water, aam panna, nimbu pani, sugarcane juice, watermelon | To cool down our body |
| **Winter** | Warm soups, stews, hot drinks, sesame sweets (til ladoo) | To keep our body warm |
| **Monsoon** | Hot pakodas, chai, corn, soups | To stay warm during rains |

**Summer fruits:** Mango, watermelon, muskmelon, litchi

**Winter fruits:** Apple, orange, guava, strawberry

> When we play or do any physical activity, we sweat and lose water from our body. That is why we should **drink water frequently**.

## Where Does Our Food Come From?

Most of our food comes from **plants**. We eat different parts of plants:

| Plant Part | Foods We Eat |
|---|---|
| **Leaves** | Spinach (palak), fenugreek (methi), cabbage, mustard leaves, coriander (dhaniya) |
| **Fruits** | Guava, apple, mango, sapota (chiku) |
| **Roots** | Carrot, radish, beetroot |
| **Stems** | Potato, ginger, onion, bamboo shoots |
| **Seeds (Grains)** | Rice, wheat, bajra, jowar, ragi |
| **Seeds (Pulses)** | Moong dal, toor dal, chana dal |
| **Seeds (Dry fruits)** | Almonds, cashews, walnuts |

## Food from Animals

Some foods come from **animals**:

| Food | Animal Source |
|---|---|
| **Milk** | Cows, buffaloes, goats |
| **Curd and paneer** | Made from milk |
| **Ghee and butter** | Made from milk |
| **Honey** | Bees |
| **Eggs** | Hens |

> **Puzzle:** Two of our most commonly used cooking ingredients are neither from plants nor from animals. Can you guess? They are **salt** (from the sea or mines) and **water**!

## Drinking Water is Essential

Our body needs a lot of water. We cannot live without water. That is why people often say, **"Water is life."**

We should drink **6 to 8 glasses of water** every day. In summer, we can also drink:

- **Tender coconut water**
- **Nimbu pani** (lime water)
- **Aam panna** (raw mango drink)
- **Buttermilk** (chaas)
- **Sugarcane juice**

---

## Do Not Waste Food

Food is precious. Many people in the world do not get enough to eat. We should:

- Take only as much food as we can eat
- Not throw away leftover food
- Share food with friends and people in need
- Eat **home-cooked food** as much as possible

> If your friend does not bring lunch to school, you should happily **share your food** with them!

---

## Key Points to Remember

- We need food to **grow, stay healthy, and get energy**.
- A **balanced diet** means eating different types of food -- grains, vegetables, fruits, pulses, milk, and nuts.
- We eat different foods in **different seasons** -- cooling foods in summer and warm foods in winter.
- Most of our food comes from **plants** -- we eat their leaves, fruits, roots, stems, and seeds.
- Some foods come from **animals** -- like milk, eggs, and honey.
- **Water** is essential for life -- we should drink plenty of water every day.
- We should **never waste food** and always share with those in need.
