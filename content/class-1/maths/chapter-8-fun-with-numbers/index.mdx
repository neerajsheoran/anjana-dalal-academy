## Understanding Tens and Ones

Every number is made of **tens** and **ones**.

- **10** is 1 ten and 0 ones.
- **23** is 2 tens and 3 ones.
- **35** is 3 tens and 5 ones.
- **47** is 4 tens and 7 ones.

We can show this using a **tens frame**. A tens frame is a box with 10 spots. For the number 23, we fill **2 full tens frames** and **3 extra spots**.

| Number | Tens | Ones |
|--------|------|------|
| 21 | 2 | 1 |
| 29 | 2 | 9 |
| 30 | 3 | 0 |
| 34 | 3 | 4 |
| 41 | 4 | 1 |
| 50 | 5 | 0 |

> **Think about it:** What are the tens and ones in the number **38**? It is **3 tens and 8 ones**!

## Finding Missing Numbers

Numbers follow an order. If some numbers are missing, we can figure them out.

- 42, **43**, 44, **45**, 46
- 77, 78, **79**, **80**, 81
- 95, 96, **97**, 98, **99**, 100

> **Count backwards too!** 50, 49, 48, 47, 46, 45...

---

## Counting Backwards

We can also count numbers from big to small. This is called **counting backwards**.

- From 30: 30, 29, 28, 27, 26, 25...
- From 50: 50, 49, 48, 47, 46, 45...
- From 100: 100, 99, 98, 97, 96, 95...

A ginladi (bead string) helps you count forwards and backwards easily.

---

## Warli Painting

**Warli painting** is a beautiful art from Maharashtra, India. It uses simple shapes like **triangles** and **circles** to draw people, trees, houses, and animals.

Artists draw tiny figures using white paint on brown walls. You can count the people, houses, and trees in a Warli painting.

> **Activity:** Try making your own Warli drawing using simple shapes!

## Key Words to Remember

| Word | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Tens** | Groups of 10 — the number 34 has 3 tens |
| **Ones** | The leftover after making groups of 10 — the number 34 has 4 ones |
| **Tens frame** | A box with 10 spots used to show numbers |
| **Hundred** | The number 100 — same as 10 tens |
| **Counting backwards** | Saying numbers from big to small (like 10, 9, 8, 7...) |
| **Ginladi** | A bead string used for counting |
| **Warli painting** | A folk art from Maharashtra using simple shapes |
