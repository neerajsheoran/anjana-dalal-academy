## Meet Pihu

Hello! My name is **Pihu**. I am 6 years old. Let me tell you about my day!

---

## Morning

The **sun rises** in the sky. It is **morning** time!

Here is what Pihu does in the morning:

1. She **wakes up** from bed.
2. She drinks a glass of **warm water**.
3. She does **yoga** or exercise.
4. She **brushes her teeth** and takes a **bath**.
5. She eats **breakfast** and gets ready for school.

> **Think about it:** What do you do in the morning?

---

## Afternoon

The **sun is high** in the sky. It is **afternoon** time!

- Pihu studies and plays with her classmates in school.
- She washes her hands and eats **lunch** with her friends.
- She comes back home from school.
- She takes **rest** for some time.

---

## Evening

The **sun is setting**. It is **evening** time!

- Pihu plays with her friends outside.
- Then she studies and does her homework.

---

## Night

The **moon** and **stars** are in the sky. It is **night** time!

- Pihu eats **dinner** with her family.
- She reads a **storybook**.
- She goes to **sleep**.

---

## Parts of the Day

The day has four main parts:

| Part of the Day | What Happens in the Sky | What We Usually Do |
|----------------|------------------------|-------------------|
| **Morning** | Sun rises | Wake up, brush, bath, breakfast |
| **Afternoon** | Sun is high up | School, lunch |
| **Evening** | Sun sets | Play, study |
| **Night** | Moon and stars appear | Dinner, sleep |

> **How to tell the time of day:** Look at the sky! If the sun is rising, it is morning. If the sun is high, it is afternoon. If the sun is going down, it is evening. If you see the moon and stars, it is night.

---

## Which Takes Longer?

Some activities take a **long time** and some take a **short time**.

| Takes Longer | Takes Shorter |
|-------------|--------------|
| Taking a bath | Brushing teeth |
| Eating a meal | Drinking a glass of water |
| Going to school | Tying your shoes |
| Sleeping at night | Washing your hands |

> **Think about it:** Does eating lunch take longer or does drinking water take longer? Eating lunch takes **longer**!

## Seasons in India

India has **four seasons**. The weather changes as the seasons change.

### Summer

- It is very **hot**.
- We wear **light cotton clothes**.
- We eat **mangoes**, watermelon, and ice cream.
- We drink lots of **water** and lemonade.

### Monsoon (Rainy Season)

- It **rains** a lot.
- We use **umbrellas** and **raincoats**.
- We see **puddles** and sometimes **rainbows**.
- The trees become very **green**.

### Winter

- It is very **cold**.
- We wear **sweaters**, jackets, and caps.
- We drink **hot milk** and eat **gajak** and **til laddoos**.
- Festivals like **Diwali** and **Christmas** come in winter.

### Spring

- The weather is **pleasant** — not too hot, not too cold.
- **Flowers bloom** on trees.
- Festivals like **Holi** and **Basant Panchami** come in spring.
- Birds sing and butterflies fly around.

## Seasons and What We Wear

| Season | What We Wear |
|--------|-------------|
| **Summer** | Cotton clothes, light colours, caps, sunglasses |
| **Monsoon** | Raincoat, umbrella, gumboots |
| **Winter** | Sweater, jacket, muffler, gloves, cap |
| **Spring** | Comfortable light clothes |

---

## Seasons and What We Eat

| Season | What We Enjoy Eating |
|--------|---------------------|
| **Summer** | Mango, watermelon, ice cream, lassi |
| **Monsoon** | Pakora, chai, corn on the cob |
| **Winter** | Gajar ka halwa, gajak, hot soup |
| **Spring** | Fresh fruits, gujiya (during Holi) |

## Key Words to Remember

| Word | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Morning** | The early part of the day when the sun rises |
| **Afternoon** | The middle part of the day when the sun is high |
| **Evening** | The later part of the day when the sun sets |
| **Night** | The dark part of the day when the moon and stars are out |
| **Season** | A time of year with its own type of weather |
| **Summer** | The hot season |
| **Monsoon** | The rainy season |
| **Winter** | The cold season |
| **Spring** | The pleasant season when flowers bloom |
